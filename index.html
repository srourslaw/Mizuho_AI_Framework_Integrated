Executive Overview

Mizuho Bank Southeast Asia stands at a pivotal moment in enterprise AI adoption. Building upon the AIX Promotion Office establishment and ongoing IBM watsonx operational reliability initiatives, this comprehensive framework addresses the fundamental challenge of translating corporate AI strategy into practical, implementable solutions across Singapore, Hong Kong, and India. Our approach focuses on Sanctions Screening Precision AI and IT Operations Reliability AI - two strategic applications that deliver immediate business value while establishing foundation capabilities for scalable AI adoption across sixteen countries.
