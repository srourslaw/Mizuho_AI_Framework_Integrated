Assumptions for Success

The following assumptions are critical for achieving the proposed timeline and deliverables. These represent industry best practices for enterprise AI implementations.

⏱️ Timeline Dependencies
Timelines are best-case scenarios assuming timely access to key Mizuho stakeholders (people), defined processes, and required technical environments (tools). Delays in stakeholder availability may extend timeline accordingly.
🔒 Data Requirements
All prototypes will be developed using synthetic or fully anonymized data provided by Mizuho. Success of the pilot phase depends on access to relevant, de-identified historical data for model validation.
🏗️ Infrastructure Access
Workshop success requires access to relevant technical environments, existing screening systems documentation, and integration specifications for proof-of-concept development and validation.
📋 Regulatory Alignment
All implementations will comply with MAS, HKMA, and RBI regulatory requirements. Any changes in regulatory landscape during engagement may require scope adjustments and timeline modifications.
